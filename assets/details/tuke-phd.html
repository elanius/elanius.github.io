I had the opportunity to become a doctoral student while working at Photoneo within the Technical University of Košice, Faculty of Electrical Engineering and Informatics, in the Department of Electrical Engineering and Mechatronics. This opportunity arose because our remote office was located directly on the university campus.

During my studies, I focused on research in the field of mobile robotics and unmanned aerial vehicles. For my dissertation thesis, I developed a drone capable of mapping and navigating in GPS-denied environments.

  • Simulation of Multiple Autonomous Mobile Robots Using a Gazebo Simulator

  • Introduction into Autonomous Mobile Robot Research and Multi Cooperation
